Dots is an all-in-one payment infrastructure platform that simplifies the complexities of payments, payouts, onboarding, and compliance. We offer businesses a seamless solution for handling global transactions.

## Benefits of Using Dots

- **Unified Platform:** Manage payments and payouts in one place.
- **Global Reach:** Send payouts to 150+ countries and in 135+ currencies.
- **Simplified Onboarding:** Quickly onboard users with staggered KYC and automated compliance checks.
- **Security and Compliance:** Automate OFAC checks, ID verification, and TIN matching to ensure regulatory compliance.
- **Customizable:** Use prebuilt flows or create custom UIs to integrate Dots smoothly into your application.

## Key Features

- **Payouts:** Offer local and global payouts to contractors and service providers with simple API integration.
- **Flexible Digital Wallets:** "Send fast global payouts programmatically with competitive rates."
- **Risk and Identity Management:** Enable instant user onboarding, simplify compliance, and effectively manage risks.
- **Strong Payment Acceptance:** Use Dots' network of payment partners to increase approval rates and lower processing expense
